Careers at SwissBorg

We aim to build a meritocratic community around a transparent and democratic investment environment where financial services will always be delivered in accordance with community expectations. We will build trust with our users around the future of crypto assets as an investment and daily banking utility.

We Believe In...

  • Proactivity rather than bureaucracy
  • Meritocracy over hierarchy
  • Being Community-centric rather than shareholder centric
  • Trust, the sine qua non condition of any human interaction
  • Creating easy access to our services
  • A well regulated environment made in Switzerland
  • Experimenting with new technologies to stay ahead of the curve
  • Creating enjoyable experiences and having fun
